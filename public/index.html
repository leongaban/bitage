What is Bitcoin

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency and payments network. Due to characteristics such as it's limited supply, and fungibility, it is akin to being digital gold.

And because of its technological advantages over gold, such as its transportability and divisibility we believe it is far superior to gold as a store of value and form of money.
